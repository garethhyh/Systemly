The problem

Routine questions are eating real hours

Most small-to-mid-sized businesses run employee and customer support out of scattered emails, spreadsheets, and shared inboxes. That works, until the volume doesn't.

Slow responses

Customers wait on fragmented channels for answers that already exist somewhere in your internal docs.

Repetitive load

How it works

Five steps, every time

Retrieval and escalation logic run as plain, auditable steps — not a black box — so each part can be tested on its own.

01

Ask

A question arrives by chat, from an employee or a customer.

02

Retrieve

Systemly pulls the matching rows from the verified knowledge base.

03

Classify

The question is sorted into IT, HR, Billing, or Sales.

04

Decide

Confident → answer immediately. Unsure → escalate to a person.

05

Log

Every decision is recorded, so the team can see what got answered.
